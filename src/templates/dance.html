Traditional Dances Around the World

Celebrating heritage and movement from different cultures.

Samba - Brazil

Samba is a lively and rhythmical dance of Afro-Brazilian origin. It is known for its festive and carnival-like atmosphere, and it's a symbol of Brazilian national identity.

Kathakali - India

Kathakali is a major form of classical Indian dance. It is a "story play" genre of art, but one distinguished by the elaborately colorful make-up, costumes and face masks that the traditionally male actor-dancers wear.
