Cultural Tourism Destinations

Kyoto, Japan

Kyoto is famous for its classical Buddhist temples, as well as its gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses. It is also known for formal traditions such as kaiseki dining, consisting of multiple courses of precise dishes, and geisha, female entertainers often found in the Gion district.
